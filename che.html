Department of Chemical Engineering

Empowering students to innovate sustainable processes for a cleaner and greener future. We blend chemical principles with modern technology to shape industry-ready engineers.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a center of excellence in chemical engineering education and research, fostering innovation for a sustainable world.

Mission

  • Deliver strong fundamentals in chemical engineering.
  • Encourage environmentally responsible and ethical practices.
  • Promote interdisciplinary research and industry collaboration.

Why Choose Chemical Engineering?

Industrial Demand

Work across petroleum, pharma, food, energy, and material sectors with high global relevance.

Sustainability Focus

Drive innovations in green energy, pollution control, and waste management.

Research & Development

Participate in transformative projects and gain experience through industry internships and academic research.
