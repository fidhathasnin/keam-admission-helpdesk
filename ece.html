Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

The ECE Department at GECK Kozhikode fosters innovation and excellence in electronics, embedded systems, and communication technology.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop competent professionals in Electronics and Communication Engineering who can innovate, lead, and contribute to societal and technological advancements.

Mission

  • Provide a strong academic foundation with an emphasis on emerging technologies.
  • Encourage innovation, interdisciplinary research, and collaboration.
  • Promote ethical engineering practices and lifelong learning among students.

Why Choose ECE?

Modern Laboratories

Advanced labs for VLSI, communication systems, microprocessors, and more, offering real-world experience.

Research & Projects

Students actively participate in funded projects and publish papers in national/international journals.

Workshops & Hackathons

Regular tech events, national-level hackathons, and technical paper presentations to boost innovation.
