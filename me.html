Department of Mechanical Engineering

Equipping students with skills in design, manufacturing, and innovation. The Mechanical Engineering department at GECK blends traditional fundamentals with cutting-edge technologies.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a center of excellence producing globally competent mechanical engineers with a strong commitment to society and sustainability.

Mission

  • Impart high-quality education in mechanical engineering.
  • Promote research and industry interaction.
  • Encourage innovation and entrepreneurship.

Why Choose Mechanical Engineering?

Core Engineering Base

Mechanical forms the foundation for multi-disciplinary roles in robotics, automation, and production systems.

Real-World Application

Design, test, and manufacture systems used in industries from automotive to aerospace.

Versatile Career Paths

Openings in design, maintenance, energy, R&D, and government sectors worldwide.
