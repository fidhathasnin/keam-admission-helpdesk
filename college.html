About Government Engineering College Kozhikode

Government Engineering College Kozhikode (GECK), established in 1999, is a government institution affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University.

Located in West Hill, Kozhikode, GECK is known for its academic excellence, modern facilities, and vibrant student community.

The college offers UG programs in AEI, Chemical, Mechanical, Civil, ECE, and CSD, along with PG courses. It actively fosters research, innovation, and entrepreneurship through IEDC and student initiatives.
