About Government Engineering College Kozhikode

Government Engineering College Kozhikode (GECK), established in 1999, is a premier institution under the Government of Kerala. It operates under the Department of Technical Education and is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University.

Located in West Hill, Kozhikode, GECK is known for its academic excellence, dedicated faculty, and vibrant student community. The campus houses modern laboratories, a central library, seminar halls, and active clubs that promote innovation and leadership.

The college offers undergraduate programs in six branches: AEI, Chemical, Mechanical, Civil, ECE, and CSD. It also hosts postgraduate programs and encourages research, industry collaboration, and entrepreneurship through IEDC and other initiatives.
