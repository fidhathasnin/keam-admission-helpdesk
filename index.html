About UDSF GECK

UDSF (United Democratic Students' Front) at Government Engineering College Kozhikode is a vibrant student organization committed to the overall growth and support of the student community.

We aim to foster unity, inclusivity, and empowerment through educational, cultural, and social initiatives. UDSF serves as a bridge between students and administration, ensuring every voice is heard and valued.

From guiding freshers to hosting events and addressing student needs, UDSF GECK is here to make your college journey meaningful and memorable.
