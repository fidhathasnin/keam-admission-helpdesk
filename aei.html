Department of Applied Electronics and Instrumentation

The AEI Department at GECK Kozhikode focuses on core instrumentation, control, and automation domains. With state-of-the-art labs and skilled faculty, students graduate industry-ready with a strong interdisciplinary foundation.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Instrumentation and Automation education, nurturing professionals capable of contributing to modern industrial solutions through innovation and technical expertise.

Mission

  • Deliver quality education in applied electronics and instrumentation.
  • Promote hands-on training and industry-relevant skillsets.
  • Encourage innovation, lifelong learning, and professional ethics.

Why Choose AEI?

Industrial Relevance

Focus on instrumentation, automation, sensors, and control systems highly valued in industries.

Project-Based Learning

Practical, application-oriented projects ensure strong technical and problem-solving skills.

Wide Career Opportunities

Graduates work in manufacturing, biomedical, aerospace, energy sectors, and core instrumentation fields.
